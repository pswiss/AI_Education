### Optimal Reconfiguration of Distributed Battery Networks Under Connectivity and Energy Constraints

The paper develops BA-DTO, a mixed-integer optimization method that reconfigures battery-powered networks by selecting Steiner-tree-based topologies that prioritize low-battery terminals while respecting strict length budgets. It also adds a topology-transition penalty that cuts unnecessary reconfiguration by 72% while still raising the minimum battery level from 2.7% to 68.6% over 30 iterations.
